ABOUT US

Non-fungible tokens (NFTs) are digital securities kept in a blockchain, a distributed ledger. An NFT's owner can transfer ownership in the blockchain, allowing NFTs to be sold and exchanged. NFTs often reference photographs, movies, and music.NFTs are distinguishable from fungible cryptocurrencies. NFTs are worth the digital file they reference.
